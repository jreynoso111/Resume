About Me

Operator, analyst, and builder of practical reporting.

I spent over 15 years inside maintenance shops, rail systems, and fleet operations before moving deeper into data and analytics. That hands-on time guides how I scope problems, clean data, and design reports: everything should be simple to read, grounded in operational reality, and ready for action.

What drives my work

  • Translating operational pain points into clear metrics and dashboards.
  • Reducing waste by pairing maintenance data with cost and inventory signals.
  • Documenting processes so improvements stick beyond a single report.

How I collaborate

  • Work directly with technicians, controllers, and managers to capture real constraints.
  • Prototype quickly in Power BI, Excel, or Python notebooks to validate ideas.
  • Communicate findings in Spanish or English depending on the team.

Focus areas

Maintenance reliability, inventory stewardship, fraud detection, and fleet utilization.

Toolkit

Power BI, DAX, Power Query, advanced Excel, Python automation, and practical SOPs that help teams adopt new reporting.
