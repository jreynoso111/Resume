About Me

Curious, grounded, and motivated by the people around me.

I'm a family-first, bilingual traveler who grew up between communities and cultures. I love hearing how people solve problems, tinkering with whatever is in front of me, and turning shared meals into a ritual that keeps loved ones close.

What matters to me

  • Creating a calm, dependable home for my kids with routines they can count on.
  • Making space for curiosity—books, podcasts, and long conversations over coffee.
  • Sharing food as a love language, whether it's a quick breakfast or a slow Sunday dinner.

How I show up

  • Listen first and reflect back what I heard so people know they're understood.
  • Stay steady and patient when plans change, looking for the next best step.
  • Switch comfortably between Spanish and English to keep everyone included.

Outside the office

I recharge with evening walks, sketching ideas in a notebook, and road trips with my family that end at a local food spot.

Roots and rhythms

Born in a big city but happiest near the water, I bring a mix of Latino warmth and practical midwestern sensibility wherever I go.
