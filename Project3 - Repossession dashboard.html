What this system does

Risk & repo operations

This project organizes everything that can go wrong with a funded vehicle into one view: missed payments, broken promises, GPS issues, suspicious movement and stalled communication with the borrower or the repo agent.

The goal is simple: at any moment, you should know which units are safe, which are deteriorating and which are ready to be recovered without guesswork.

  • Tracks the full lifecycle of a risky deal: soft warning → hard collections → repo → storage → sale.
  • Combines financial status (arrears, fees, promises) with GPS behavior and agent reports.
  • Standardizes status definitions, so everyone speaks the same language when talking about risk.

Repo & risk pipeline

From “late” to “recovered”

Every unit is forced into a clear stage. No “grey zone” units that nobody is actively managing.

  • Current status (On time · At risk · Recovery · Repo complete · Sold).
  • Last key event (missed payment, promise broken, GPS tampered, agent assignment, pickup, auction).
  • Days in stage and time since last action by internal or external teams.

That structure makes it obvious where money is getting stuck: delayed assignments, idle repos, units sitting in yards or auctions longer than they should.

Key KPIs & signals

What gets monitored
  • Units 30/60/90+ days late by portfolio, region, vendor and agent.
  • Average time from “assign to agent” to “unit recovered”.
  • Units with GPS offline, tampered, or parked out of contract area.
  • Storage days and charges by yard and by unit.
  • Recovered units pending sale, title, or balance resolution.
