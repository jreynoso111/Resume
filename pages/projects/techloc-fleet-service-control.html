TechLoc Fleet & Service Control Platform

TechLoc is a logistics platform designed to optimize fleet management and nationwide technical service coordination. Its core mission is to instantly connect fleet operators with the right service providers at the exact location where support is needed.

Platform Overview

TechLoc centralizes geolocation, dispatch orchestration, provider validation, and performance analytics into one operational command center. Fleet teams can request services, assign certified partners, and monitor outcomes in real time without switching between fragmented tools.

Key Functionalities

  • Smart Localization: Geo-intelligent search and map-based control view to locate technicians, tow trucks, and workshops in real time.
  • Dispatch Management: Rapid coordination of roadside assistance, heavy-equipment transport, and commercial locksmith services.
  • Analytics Panel: Live metrics for active partner status, SLA compliance, response times, and operational efficiency.
  • Curated Network: Verified provider database that ensures speed, quality, and trusted execution in every intervention.

Emergency Dispatch Workflows

Roadside Assistance

Trigger service tickets by location, assign nearest certified partner, and track ETA-to-resolution in a single timeline.

Heavy Equipment Transport

Coordinate towing and specialized transport with route visibility, status checkpoints, and centralized documentation.

Commercial Locksmith

Accelerate lockout resolution through geo-filtered provider selection and SLA-prioritized dispatch logic.

Operational Impact

By combining smart geolocation, dispatch intelligence, and curated provider quality controls, TechLoc reduces response times, improves SLA performance, and gives fleet leaders a clear nationwide operational picture. The platform turns service coordination into a measurable, data-driven workflow with stronger control and better customer outcomes.
