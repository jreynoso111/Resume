I am a maintenance and asset operations professional focused on structured systems, operational control, fleet oversight, and inventory integrity. I build dependable operating routines that prioritize reliability, discipline, measurable performance, and consistent execution.

In my current role in New York, I oversee inventory and GPS systems for approximately 260 assets to keep fleet visibility accurate and operational readiness high. I also coordinate closely with vendors and align external teams to maintain service continuity, data integrity, and clear operating standards.

Biography & Education

Before concentrating on asset and fleet operations, I built experience in infrastructure systems across a metro environment, including SCADA, CCTV, access control, and intrusion systems. That work strengthened my ability to manage high-dependability operations where precision, response, and system continuity matter every day.

My foundation includes academic training in technical and systems-based disciplines, with coursework in project methodologies that shaped how I plan, document, and execute work with consistency.

Philosophy

My leadership philosophy is grounded in structure, accountability, and measurable standards. I make calm decisions under pressure, define clear ownership, and focus on repeatable processes that improve reliability without unnecessary complexity.

Personal Life

I was born in Santo Domingo in 1987 and raised by my mother alongside my siblings, an upbringing that taught me resilience, responsibility, and quiet discipline. I have always felt a deep connection to nature and the sea, and immigrating to New York became an important part of my growth and development, both personally and professionally.
