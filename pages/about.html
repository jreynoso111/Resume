Juan Radhamés Reynoso Pérez is a maintenance and fleet operations leader focused on asset reliability, preventive maintenance, and inventory control.

His approach centers on "clean answers to messy problems," using data, analytics, and automation to support maintenance decisions, control costs, and keep critical assets available for service.

Biography & Education

Reynoso developed an early interest in how complex systems function. He pursued technical education at the Universidad Autónoma de Santo Domingo (UASD), where he completed coursework toward a Higher Technology degree in Food Engineering.

That training strengthened his discipline in:

•
Process control, quality assurance standards (ISO), and maintenance consistency.
•
Systematic analysis of inputs, outputs, and asset lifecycle behavior.
•
Operational discipline required for industrial maintenance and reliability environments.

Philosophy

Reynoso emphasizes clear ownership, reliable maintenance planning, and automation that supports frontline technicians and supervisors. His goal is simple: make the right operational action obvious at the right time.

Personal Life

Outside of professional systems, Reynoso is an avid photographer, focusing on urban textures and natural landscapes. He is also a proponent of camping and outdoor activities as a method for mental clarity and "resetting" from digital environments.
