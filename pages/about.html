The Person Behind the Data

An active mind, driven by values and a history of solving complex problems.

I am Juan Radhamés Reynoso Pérez. Born in Santo Domingo in September 1987, I come from a close-knit family that instilled in me a strong sense of integrity and the importance of doing good work.

I currently live in New York, having moved back a year ago, but my professional character was forged during 16 years of service at the Santo Domingo Metro.

The Metro Era (16 Years)

My relationship with the Santo Domingo Metro began very early in the project's history. I started at the Central Control as a Technical Assistant, but my curiosity and work ethic quickly opened new doors.

After my first year, I was transferred to the Maintenance Department, specifically focusing on Telecommunications, Signaling, and Ticketing. Over the next decade and a half, this wasn't just a job—it was a school of life. I gained deep technical training in railway security systems, installation operations, and quality control.

It was here that I developed my passion for analytics. Managing maintenance control and anti-fraud systems taught me that data isn't just numbers—it's the heartbeat of an operation.

Education & Growth

I am a lifelong learner with an active mind that never stays still. I hold a degree in Higher Technology in Food Engineering from the Autonomous University of Santo Domingo (UASD).

While my career pivoted toward transportation and data technology, that engineering background gave me a rigorous foundation in process management, safety standards, and project structure that I apply to everything I do today.

Who I Am

I am a person who is always trying to learn something new. Whether it is solving a complex inventory problem or learning a new software tool, I thrive on challenges.

Moving back to New York has been a new chapter, bringing my experience from the Dominican Republic to new environments, blending my technical history with the fast-paced culture of NYC.

Passions
