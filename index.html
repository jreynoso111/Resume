Summary

Professional profile

My background combines hands‑on maintenance management, fraud detection, and data analytics. I specialize in turning operational chaos into structured, decision‑ready reporting that exposes blind spots, highlights cost drivers, and supports reliable planning. I bridge the gap between field operations and data workflows so managers understand not just the numbers, but the underlying operational story. I understand both the spreadsheets and the reality in the yard, workshop or station, and I build reporting with that operational context in mind.
